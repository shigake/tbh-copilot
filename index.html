TBH CO·PILOT
100% LOCAL

An optimization companion for the idle-RPG TBH: Task Bar Hero. It reads your save locally in your browser, decrypts it, and tells you the optimal next move: where to farm, when to come back, when you'll level, and which runes and gear to get.

Drop your save. Read your next move.

Open the app Source ★ GitHub
FARM

Every cleared stage ranked by gold/hour and exp/hour from your real clear speed — each wearing its monster's face.

RUNE TREE

All 197 nodes, with the cheapest buyable picks for the goal you choose.

ROSTER & GEAR

POWER, DPS and EHP per hero, plus the power delta of any gear swap — and where it drops.

IDLE

The best time to come back, and what to leave running while you're away.

Your save never leaves your computer. Everything is read and decrypted in your browser, with no servers and no tracking. Works in any modern browser — just pick your save file and done.
